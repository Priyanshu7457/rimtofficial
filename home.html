RIMT at a glance
Rotary Institute of Management and Technology (RIMT) is ranked amongst the top educational institutions affiliated to M. J. P. Rohilkhand University, Bareilly. Established in 2008, the institute has achieved a status of distinction by following the path envisioned by Dr. Bhagwati Prasad, Academic Adviser at Invertis University, Bareilly, whose grandeur and mature guidance has realized the dream cherished by the honorable members of the Rotary Club Chandausi. The present glorious status of RIMT is due to the strenuous efforts of its founder members - Rtn. Dinesh Agrawal (President), Rtn. Pramod Agrawal (secretary), Rtn. Krishna Mohan (treasurer).


RIMT is a dream prodigy of dedicated members of Rotary Club of Chandausi. It imparts a vision to young generation to lead the country into national and international challenging environment. It believes in producing quality man-power equipped with human and social values required to integrate excellent technical skill, social character, leadership, creativity and innovation for the benefit of mankind and sustainable development of the nation. The credit of its spectacular growth goes to its commitment to providing world class education, with curriculum designed with the help of industry experts with a team of highly qualified and dedicated faculty with academic intelligence and industry exposure. Its infrastructure has been developed in a planned and systematic manner as per the requirements of modern professional institutions to support high standards of teaching, learning and development of overall personality.

RIMT’s striking features are its well maintained spacious and air conditioned class rooms, transportation facility, well stocked library, well equipped and air conditioned computer lab ,E-learning facility, sports and recreational aids, English classes, personality development classes, industrial and educational tours, canteen facility , placement assistance and many more. Besides this, for the development in students’ personality the Institute organizes various activities like Quiz competitions and debates, workshops and seminars, group discussions and guest lectures by eminent professionals in various fields. Along with this, RIMT avails its students of the opportunities to participate in the activities held in other reputed institutions of the nation. RIMT itself provides them a big platform in the name of ‘RIMT SPIRIT’ to enlighten and flourish their inherent talents. ‘RIMT SPIRIT’ is annual festival of sports and cultural activities. It has become a great source of encouragement for the students and to inculcate in them a 360 degree personality development.

RIMT presently offers education in the professional courses-B.B.A. and B.C.A. But in order to fulfil the accelerating demand of traditional courses along with professional ones, RIMT is going to impart education in the courses-B.A., B. Com., B. Sc. and B.Ed. It is also going to start the courses -B.com.(Hons.) and B.sc.(Home Science) from the next session. Lush Green Campus RIMT is situated at Sita Ashram Road, Pathra Tiraha, Chandausi. The infrastructure of the institute has been developed in a planned and systematic manner as per the requirement of modern professional institutes. Greenery and varied plantation adds beauty to the infrastructure and helps in maintaining conducive atmosphere needed for studious pursuits and supports the high standards of teaching, learning and development of overall personality of the students.
